WELCOME HOME!

PALIA

Published by Wiki.gg
Aug.01,2023

Palia is a cozy community simulator game developed and published by Singularity 6. Palia was released on PC on August 2, 2023, Epic Games.svg Epic Games Store on October 24, 2023, Nintendo Switch.svg Nintendo Switch on December 14, 2023, and Steam.svg Steam on March 25, 2024.

Summary

Carve out a home of your own as you explore a vibrant world filled with welcoming villagers and beautiful landscapes. Everything from sprawling flower fields to hilly forests and rocky beaches lie at your feet. Make your home unique by decorating and customizing your space with hundreds of craftable items. A friendly, cozy community is always here to welcome you home. Play solo or jump in with friends at any time to play at your own pace.

Story

In Palia, humans are the legendary race, having disappeared thousands of years ago at the height of their grandeur and mastery of magic. Nobody in the modern day knows why. One day, humans begin to reappear all over the world. As one of the newly re-emerged humans, players will have to make themselves a new home in this friendly world, and forge a future for themselves and humanity.[1]
